Hi there I'm Kafka, a drawer from [www.giantessnight.com](http://www.giantessnight.com)  

Here I try to post a set of manga which is drawn by myself while based on the novel with the same name written by Dante, the administrator of GN website.
This set of manga is still to be continued,and hence the three chapters are not drawn in the same period of time (Chapter1 in Aug 2009,Chapter2 in Dec 2009,Chapter3 in Feb 2010),the style of drawing charactors may be a bit difference.... I'll try to improve my drawskill(Also my English expression).

Unfortunately my poor English is still uncapable to translate this set of manga,but maybe you can understand what happened by the pictures.
This manga mainly tells about a elfy giantess which doesn't belonged to any species of the giantess in the universe.Her name is Shivir.Shivir has forgotten all the memory about where she born,where her motherland is ,and who she is,only has endless nightmares about a giga giantess goddess that looks definitely like her distroys planets and crushes all the livings just for fun.

Shivir traveled for thousands of years tring to find out the answer to these mystics until she met Jack Javis,a mage who is wanted for murdering a president of a planet.But actually Jack isn't the real murderor.Shivir rescured Jack and took him to her spacecraft.As a requite to her favor,Jack records Shivir's nightmares and displays them for Shivir to find any useful information about where Shivir comes.However,someone of the Holy See has a great conspiracy to them... <title>12</title> <link href="../Styles/Style.css" type="text/css" rel="stylesheet">
